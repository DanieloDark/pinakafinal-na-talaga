Who We Are

We are BSCS 2-4 students from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Manila, batch 2023-2024. This project is part of our Information Management System coursework. Our goal is to develop innovative solutions that streamline complex processes and make them more user-friendly.

Overview of the Project

The BPI Family Housing Loan Management System offers tailored options specifically designed for different housing loan purposes. This project aims to create a Database Management System (DBMS) that replicates the functions of the BPI Housing Loan Application Form. This form, used by Bank of the Philippine Islands (BPI), gathers essential applicant details related to their eligibility for a housing loan. Its purpose is to collect details about the applicant, property, loan purpose, and financial information. Additionally, this allows the Bank of the Philippines Islands to determine the applicant's eligibility for a loan with speed and accuracy in the approval process. The project also aims to enhance transparency in loan terms and conditions through a detailed FAQ section and real-time application updates. Applicants are required to strictly adhere to BPI's business guidelines for accurate completion of required fields and document submission.
